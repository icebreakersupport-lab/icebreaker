Icebreaker Live

Privacy Policy

Icebreaker Live is built to help people meet nearby in real time — respectfully and safely. This policy explains every category of data we collect, why we collect it, who we share it with, and how long we keep it. We believe you deserve a straight answer to every one of those questions.

Last updated: May 22, 2026

Age requirement

Icebreaker Live is strictly for users who are 18 years of age or older. We collect your date of birth during account setup to verify eligibility. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate their account and delete their data. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at icebreaker.support@gmail.com.

What we collect

Account and identity

  • Email address and hashed password (when using email/password sign-up).
  • Apple ID token and associated email when you use Sign in with Apple. Apple may provide a privacy-relay email address at your option.
  • Date of birth (used for age verification; not shown to other users).

Profile information

  • Display name, gender identity, and sexual orientation (used for discovery filters; you control what is visible on your profile).
  • Profile photos uploaded from your camera or photo library.
  • Bio, preferences, discovery filter settings, and other profile fields you choose to complete.

Live-session data

  • Real-time selfie photo captured when you start a live session ("Go Live") — used as your live avatar and for identity verification during the session.
  • Precise GPS latitude and longitude, encoded as a geohash, while a live session is active.
  • Session status (live, ended), session timestamps, and associated session records.

Interactions and communications

  • Icebreaker messages you send and receive.
  • Chat messages exchanged after a mutual post-meet agreement.
  • Meetup color-match state and post-meet feedback.
  • Block and report records you create or are subject to.

Device and technical data

  • Device push notification token (used to deliver push notifications you have enabled).
  • IP address (collected by Firebase infrastructure as part of normal network requests).
  • Device type and OS version (collected automatically by Firebase Analytics for crash reporting and diagnostics).

Purchase data

  • In-app purchase transaction IDs (Apple App Store or Google Play receipt data) forwarded to our Cloud Function to verify and grant credits.
  • Your current Icebreaker Credit and Live Session Credit balances.
  • Rewarded ad completion events (from Google AdMob) used to grant earned credits.

Permissions we may request

  • Camera — required to take your live verification selfie and optional profile photos.
  • Photo Library — required only if you choose to upload photos from your device gallery for your profile.
  • Location (Precise, When In Use) — required while a live session is active to determine who is nearby.
  • Push Notifications — optional; used to alert you to icebreakers, messages, match confirmations, and session events.

How location works in Go Live

When you start a live session, Icebreaker uses your device's precise location to show you nearby people and show you to them. Your exact latitude and longitude are used server-side to calculate proximity, but your exact coordinates are never displayed as a readout to other users. Other users see only that you are live and nearby.

Important: if you leave the app during an active live session, your last known live location may still be used until the session ends or you stop it. This keeps your live session visible rather than incorrectly appearing offline the moment your phone backgrounds the app.

If you do not want to remain visible, end your live session. When a session ends, the session location fields are cleared from active discovery. We do not request background location access — location is only accessed while the app is in use during a live session.

How we use your information

  • To create, authenticate, and manage your account.
  • To power nearby discovery, live sessions, icebreakers, meetups, chats, and color-match features.
  • To verify that you are present and real during a live session (live selfie).
  • To send transactional push notifications — new icebreakers, messages, match confirmations, session events, and reminders — based on your notification preferences.
  • To send account-related emails via Resend, including email verification messages.
  • To detect abuse, investigate reports, and support moderation and account safety systems.
  • To process in-app purchases and update your credit balances.
  • To serve rewarded advertisements and grant credits upon ad completion.
  • To improve the reliability and performance of the App through crash data and diagnostics.

Sensitive personal information

Certain information you provide — including your gender identity and sexual orientation — is considered sensitive personal data under various privacy laws. We collect this information solely to power discovery filters that let you and other users control who appears in nearby searches and who can see you.

We do not sell, share for advertising purposes, or use this data for any purpose other than operating the discovery features described above.

Who can see your information

  • Other users can see the profile information you choose to include — name, photos, bio, and any tags you add.
  • Other users can see that you are live and nearby while your live session is active. They see your live selfie avatar, not your exact location.
  • Other users' discovery filter preferences may cause your profile to be hidden from them, or vice versa, without either party being notified.
  • Other users do not receive your exact GPS coordinates at any time.

Advertising and Google AdMob

The "Earn Free" section of our Shop displays rewarded video advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use your device's advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, Google Advertising ID on Android), IP address, and ad interaction data in accordance with Google's own privacy policies.

On iOS, AdMob participation is subject to Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You may see a prompt asking whether to allow tracking. You can change this setting at any time in your device's Privacy & Security settings.

Icebreaker Live does not display non-rewarded ads. Ads only appear in the Shop when you choose to watch one to earn credits.

Note: Opting out of ad tracking limits AdMob's ability to show you personalized ads, but you can still watch rewarded ads and earn credits.

Notifications

If you allow notifications, Icebreaker may send you alerts about live-session activity, incoming icebreakers, messages, match outcomes, and reminders. You can control which notification categories are enabled inside the app at any time. You can also revoke notification permission entirely through your device settings.

Data retention

  • Account and profile data — retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Live-session location data — cleared from active discovery when a session ends. Session records are retained for a limited period for safety and moderation purposes, then deleted.
  • Messages — retained while your account is active. Deleted along with your account upon deletion.
  • Report and block records — retained for a limited period after account deletion to support safety and moderation.
  • Purchase records — transaction IDs may be retained for legal compliance and financial record-keeping as required by law.
  • Crash and diagnostic logs — retained for a limited period (typically 90 days) per Firebase's standard policies.

Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — you may update your profile information at any time within the app.
  • Deletion — you may delete your account and associated data at any time (see Account deletion below).
  • Data portability — you may request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out of notifications — you can disable any or all notification categories in the app or through device settings.
  • Ad tracking opt-out — you can limit ad tracking through your device's privacy settings.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at icebreaker.support@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Account deletion

You can delete your account inside the app through Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Deletion is permanent and removes your profile, photos, messages, credits, and all associated Firestore and Storage data. If you no longer have the app installed, you can use our deletion page instead.

Children's privacy (COPPA)

Icebreaker Live is not directed at, and does not knowingly collect personal information from, children under 13 — or from anyone under 18. We enforce an 18+ age requirement at account creation. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will immediately terminate that account and delete their data. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us immediately at icebreaker.support@gmail.com.

Security

We use Firebase's security infrastructure, Firestore Security Rules, and Firebase Storage Rules to protect your data. All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Account credentials are managed by Firebase Authentication — we never store plaintext passwords.

No system is 100% secure. If you suspect your account has been compromised, change your password immediately and contact us.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or by email before the changes take effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when the policy was last revised. Your continued use of the App after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
