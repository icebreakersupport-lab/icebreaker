How it works

From the same room
to a real conversation.

Icebreaker is built for the moment you're in — a bar, campus, coffee shop, concert, event. No endless swiping. No chat purgatory. Just a respectful, fast path to a real-life interaction.

1
Go Live

Tap Go Live and take a quick selfie. You're now visible to nearby people who are also live. Your live session runs for a set window — you're in control.

2
See who's nearby

Browse real people who are live, nearby, and open to meeting right now. Only mutual-opt-in people can see each other — no uninvited contact.

3
Send one icebreaker

Pick one person, send one icebreaker message. Make it count. If they accept, you both get a shared color to find each other IRL.

4
Meet in person

Find your color, have a short in-person conversation. If you both say yes after meeting, chat unlocks. The spark happens in person — not on a screen.

Why Icebreaker

A new way
to play the game.

📷
Live verification selfie

Every active session requires a real-time selfie. No catfishing, no stale profiles — you see the person as they are right now, right there.

📍
Proximity-first matching

Only people who are physically nearby and actively live appear on your radar. This is about the moment you're in, not someone three cities away.

⚡
Mutual consent, always

Both people must opt in before any meeting happens. One icebreaker, one chance — no harassment, no spam, no unwanted contact.

💬
Chat unlocks after meeting

The app doesn't let you disappear into a chat thread. Meet first, vibe first — then both decide whether to keep talking.

🛠
Block, report, control

Instantly block anyone, report bad behavior, and manage exactly who can see you. Safety is built into every layer — not bolted on as an afterthought.

✨
Respectful by design

Icebreaker isn't designed to help you match — it's designed to help you meet. The spark happens in person. We just make it less awkward to start.

Safety

Respectful and safe —
that's the whole game.

Icebreaker is built with real-world safety at its core. Mutual opt-in before any contact. Live selfie verification on every session. One-tap blocking and reporting. Age-verified accounts (18+). We enforce the rules — so you can focus on the conversation.
